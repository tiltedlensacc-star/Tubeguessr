Privacy Policy for TubeGuesser

Last Updated: November 14, 2024

Introduction

TubeGuesser ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application TubeGuesser (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

TubeGuesser stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game Statistics: Your game history, scores, guesses, win/loss records, and completion times
  • Game Progress: Current game state and daily game status
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active premium subscription

This data is stored locally on your device using iOS's UserDefaults and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information
  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do NOT track your location beyond what's necessary for the game functionality
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties

In-App Purchases

TubeGuesser offers premium subscriptions through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All subscription and payment processing is handled directly by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Apple's privacy policy applies to all In-App Purchase transactions: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers or databases that store your information. Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

Children's Privacy

TubeGuesser does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to use.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all app data by deleting the TubeGuesser app from your device
  • Reinstalling the app will start with fresh data
  • We cannot recover your data once deleted as we do not store it on our servers

Third-Party Services

TubeGuesser uses Apple's StoreKit framework for subscription management. Apple's privacy policy applies to this service: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access your data (all data is visible within the app)
  • Right to deletion (delete the app from your device)
  • Right to portability (data is stored locally on your device)

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Union:

  • We do not process any personal data as defined by GDPR
  • All data remains on your local device
  • You have full control over your data through device settings

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not collect personal information as defined by CCPA
  • All data is stored locally on your device
